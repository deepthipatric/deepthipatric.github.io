Projects

Selected applied work in spatial data science, earth observation, disaster risk, and participatory planning.

01

Peri-Urban Land-Use Change & Livelihood Typologies — Mankweng, South Africa

Core PhD research integrating very high resolution WorldView-3 imagery, Landsat time-series, and socio-economic datasets to map and classify peri-urban land-use change in Mankweng, Limpopo. Applied object-based image analysis (OBIA) and machine learning clustering to derive settlement typologies and livelihood patterns. Results fed directly into participatory scenario planning workshops with community and municipal stakeholders.

Machine Learning OBIA Time-Series Analysis WorldView-3 Landsat Scenario Simulation
02

Kole Wetlands Drone Mapping — Kerala, India

Led UAV-based photogrammetric mapping of the 4,200-hectare Kole Wetlands in Kerala — a Ramsar-listed wetland and critical agricultural landscape. Produced high-resolution orthomosaics and digital elevation models used for agricultural sustainability assessments and hydrological modelling. Integrated drone outputs with satellite data to analyse seasonal flooding dynamics and land-use pressures on the wetland ecosystem.

UAV / Drone Photogrammetry Hydrological Modelling DEM Sustainability
03

COVID-19 Dialysis Routing — Mumbai, India

Developed real-time, spatially explicit safe-route maps for dialysis patients during the COVID-19 lockdown in Mumbai, in direct collaboration with Mumbai Municipal Corporation. Integrated hospital locations, road network data, and containment-zone boundaries to identify optimal routes that minimized exposure risk. Maps were used operationally by the city administration to coordinate patient transport.

Network Analysis Spatial Optimization GIS Public Health Decision Support
04

Flood & Drought Risk Analysis — Kerala & South India

Designed and led state-level disaster preparedness workflows covering flood inundation modelling, drought prediction, landslide susceptibility mapping, and dynamic web-based alert systems. Used NASA hydrological frameworks and satellite-derived rainfall and soil moisture data. Outputs were used by local government agencies and integrated into community-based early warning systems in high-risk regions.

Hydrological Modelling Flood Prediction Drought Analysis Landslide Susceptibility WebGIS NASA Frameworks
05

Participatory Governance Mapping — Limpopo, South Africa

Co-produced spatial knowledge with communities, municipal planners, women's groups, and traditional leadership across multiple field visits to Limpopo. Led participatory mapping sessions and stakeholder workshops as part of the WoPedyP (Women's Perceptions and Place Dynamics) project. Ground-truthed remote sensing classifications and used community-generated data to co-develop land-based livelihood pathways and future scenario narratives for peri-urban governance planning.

Participatory Mapping Stakeholder Engagement Fieldwork Governance Community Research
06

Spatial Infrastructure Planning — K-FON, Kerala

Led GIS-based network modelling for the Kerala Fibre Optic Network (K-FON), a state-wide broadband infrastructure project. Developed spatial decision-support tools integrating OpenStreetMap, crowd-sourced, and utility network datasets to optimise cable routing and coverage. Also contributed to power grid planning using spatial optimization models across regional utility datasets.

Network Modelling Spatial Optimization OpenStreetMap WebGIS Infrastructure
