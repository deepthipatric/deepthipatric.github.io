About

I am a spatial data scientist and geographer with a PhD (cum laude) in Geography from Georg-August-Universität Göttingen. My work sits at the intersection of earth observation, machine learning, and sustainability research — combining satellite time-series analysis, UAV data, and socio-economic datasets to understand how landscapes change and how communities adapt.

I have worked across academia, government, and the private sector in India, Germany, and South Africa, contributing to projects in disaster risk management, urban land-use analysis, infrastructure planning, and participatory governance. I am passionate about applying data-driven spatial methods to real-world environmental and social challenges.
